About The Event

Dear Students,


The Spotlight Event is our college's premier cultural week where we celebrate diversity, creativity, and unity.


We invite all students to come dressed as per the theme each day wholeheartedly. Let’s make every day colorful, fun, and unforgettable!


Whether you're rocking vintage styles on Retro Day, matching outfits on Twinning Day, appreciating Middle Eastern elegance, or celebrating your roots on Traditional Day, this is your time to shine.

Let the SPOTLIGHT shine on YOU! 🎉
